Polio Virus Attacks Again!

Those who survived after battling polio were hospitalized and left paralyzed. One of the most famous people to have been affected by this virus was President Franklin Delano Roosevelt, better known as FDR, who contracted the potentially fatal virus in 1921. For almost the whole rest of his life, FDR would either walk with steel braces or sit in a wheelchair.

Hurricane Ian Hits Florida!

On September 23rd, a tropical depression formed near southeast Jamaica. In under 24 hours, it formed into a category 3 hurricane. This hurricane has destroyed cities, landmarks, and homes and it took the lives of many people.

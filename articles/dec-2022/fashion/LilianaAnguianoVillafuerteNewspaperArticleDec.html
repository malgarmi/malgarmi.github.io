Is Online Fashion Worth it

By: Liliana Anguiano , Isabella Edwards.

During 2020, a lot of small businesses were failing, and some still are. Now in 2022, a lot of small businesses get a bunch of support, and some are a well known online shop.This article is about online small businesses, and what they sell. (Most of these businesses are more girly, but we’ll try looking for businesses that sell guy clothing) The 5 small businesses that we’ll be ranking are,  Soulstylessshop, Gemsbymadeline, TheGurlyBoutique ,Offtheradarclo, Jayinc.

                                       

(5) Soulstylessshop

Soulstylessshop is an alright business. The owner of this small shop is named Stephany Andrea. She is from New York and she thrifts good 2000’s clothing, and sells it in her online shop. There are many things in her online shop, but she is mostly known for her Tracksuits, and her Bundles that she sometimes does. Since she goes to a thrift store to get her clothing, she doesn’t always have a lot of things in stock, but she has more  feminine things, and really nothing for guys. Although she has a really good online shop, and also sells really good things for the low, I’m gonna rate her business 6.5/10. The reason why I’m rating her business so low is because she sometimes has the same clothes, she doesn’t really sell clothing a lot, and her business is just really dry.

https://www.soulstylesshop.com/

(4) Gemsbymadeline

This business has a lot of cute clothing for girls. They have pants that seem really popular, and a lot of cute leggings as well. They’re instagram is Gemsbymadeline. This business has clothing that is a more fashionable type of wear, but  I definitely recommend it if you want different types of clothing. Although I really like this business, I’m gonna rate it a 7.00/10 because the  clothes that are well known are sold out. They also have the prices too expensive. One pair of their sweatpants is $120 plus tax and shipping! But other than that, they have some really good pants/sweatpants.

https://gemsbymadeline.com/

(3) TheGurlyBoutique

TheGurlyBoutique is a girl fashion website with 3 different things. They have earrings, Bikinis, and Track suits. Although they have only 3 things on their website, the clothing is really cute. I really like this brand and I rate it a 8.5/10. The website could have more things, and the prices are alright. Some of their things are kind of pricey. A bikini is $40, and then their dresses are $36.99.

https://thegurlyboutique.com/

(2) Offtheradarclo

This brand is really cool, and their  clothing that they sell is early 2000’s cyber Y2K, a type of style.  They have girl clothing, men clothing and unisex clothing. Most of the clothing is merch, but in a cool way. Their clothing looks expensive and material that isn’t cheap. They also have accessories, purses and belts. I really recommend this brand if you like their cyber Y2K style. I rate this website a 9.5/10. The website is really cool, and the clothing looks really good but it’s not the best website ever.

https://www.offtheradarclo.com/

(1) Jayinc

This website/brand is close to the best. They have phone cases, clothing, lashes, hats, croc charms and jewelry. The clothing is really cute, and also trendy. The hats are really cool. If you love bad bunny, this website is perfect for you. They have a Bad Bunny section and also other famous people on shirts! The brand isn’t perfect but it’s pretty close to it. I’m gonna rate it a 9.8/10. The website has a lot of things on it, and basically anything a girl needs. They don’t have any guy clothing, but they have unisex clothing. They don’t sell out a lot, and they have a lot of clearance  products. They also have a “Become a Brand Ambassador” under their page (A brand ambassador is when you promote a business by wearing their clothing).

https://jayincc.com/

Did any of these websites catch your attention? Hopefully you found a new website to shop on, or even just look at! All of these websites were really good, but all things have pros and cons. Some of these websites were the best, and some weren’t even close to good.

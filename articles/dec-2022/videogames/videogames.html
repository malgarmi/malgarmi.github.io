Top 10 Video Games of 2022

By Gisele L.

Do you enjoy playing video games? Video games have gained popularity over the years. They have been popular since the mid 1900’s and still are today. Over the years, technology has gotten more complex and superior than ever before! Today, thousands of online users are playing video games each day. Video games can be played on PC, computers, console, and mobile devices.  In this article, I am ranking the Top 10 Video Games of 2022 from the least to best.

10. Fall Guys

Fall Guys was released August 4, 2020. The game was created by Mediatonic - a British video game developer in London. The objective of Fall Guys is to reach the finish line going through a course of obstacles. “The game involves up to 60 players who control jellybean-like creatures and compete against each other in a series of randomly selected mini-games, such as obstacle courses…” Players also have the opportunity to create their characters. Fall Guys is also a family friendly game for the whole family. Family or friends can compete with you as well! I recommend Fall Guys being a good game because it allows you to go through obstacles and have a chance to win at the end of the course!

9. League of Legends

League of Legends was released October 27, 2009. The video game was created by Riot Games(video game developer and publisher). The objective of the game is to battle with your team to defeat the opposing enemy team and destroy towers that guard the enemy base. There are 3 game modes: PvP (Player vs Player), Co-op vs. Al, and Custom. There are also different ranks, ranking least to most: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, and Challenger. Overall, the game has players that have unique abilities and traits. League of Legends is a great video game because it allows players the opportunity to fight with your team to defeat the enemy team. The game is overall really fun and players get to play with their friends as well.

8.Valorant

Valorant has been released as of June 2, 2020. Valorant was created by Riot Games - “... an American video game developer, publisher and esports tournament organizer.” The objective is to plant and defend the spike. The game is a 5v5 with two sides on the map, attack and defense. The attacking side recieves a point if the bomb is defused. The defending side recieves a point if the bomb goes off successfully without the attacking side being able to defuse it. The game consists of four different game modes: Competitive, Unrated, Spike Rush, and Deathmath. There are not only different game modes, but also different maps: Ascent, Bind, Haven, Icebox, and Split. If you want to get competitive there are ranks that you can compete for XP to increase your rank. I believe Valorant is a good video game because it allows players to play a variety of different game modes and maps.

7.Call of Duty: Vanguard

Call of Duty: Vanguard came out November 5, 2021. The video game was created by “Sledgehammer Games, Inc. is an American video game developer company formed in 2009 by Glen Schofield and Michael Condrey.” The objective of the game is to unlock new areas around Fountain Square(main base of operation), and you can upgrade by unlocking the areas. The game consists of nine different game modes: Free for All, Team Deathmatch, Domination, Kill Confirmed, Patrol, Hardpoint, Search and Destroy, Control, and Champion Hill. Call of Duty is a great game because it gives players the ability to unlock new areas/levels to explore even more!

6. NBA 2K 23

NBA 2k 23 was released September 8, 2022. The basketball video game was created by Visual Concepts, “An American video game developer… the company is best known for developing sports games in the 2k franchise…” The objective of NBA 2k 23 is to score more baskets than the opposing team. If you score more points than the opposing team, you get to level up. There are ten different game modes: Jordan Challenge, My Career, Park, ProAm, AnteUp, BlackTop, MyTeam, PlayNow Online, The W, and MyGM. For example, MyTeam is about playing a 3v5 or 5v5 online against players to gain MT and XP rewards to boost your level. NBA 2k23 is a video game that is good because it allows players that enjoy basketball to play the sport in a video game which makes it incredibly interesting!

5. Apex Legends

Apex Legends was released February, 2019. Respawn Environment developed Apex Legends. Respawn Environment is a “... LLC American video game development studio founded by Jason West and Vince Zampella.” The objective of the game is to be the last team standing. The game will begin at a drop ship over flying the map where you and your team will pick a landing spot. Once you pick a spot, the goal is to gather loot and fight the enemy squads. Apex Legends is a online multiplayer video game, so you can play with your friends too! There are six types of game modes: Training, Firing Range, Battle Royale, Arena, Ranked Arenas. The game mode has up to 60 players to begin with, and the map begins to get smaller and teams have to search for loot to survive and kill enemies. Apex Legends is a great game because the concept of grabbing loot and fighting enemies with the team is thrilling.

4. God Of War Ragnarök

God of War Ragnarök was released November 9, 2022. God of War was developed by Santa Monica Studio: “An American video game developer based in Los Angeles.” The game was originally based on Norse mythology: a set of beliefs and stories by Northern Germanic tribes. The story behind the game was that Kratos (the protagonist) is bonding with Atreus (his son) and teaches his son how to be a god, while his son teaches Kratos how to be a human. The gods and goddesses also fight in the harsh winter environment. God of War Ragnarök is a good game because it allows players to fight with gods/goddesses in mythology.

3. Minecraft

Minecraft has been around since May 17, 2009 and is still one of the many top games played today. “The first edition of Minecraft, called Java Edition, was made by Notch during a weekend in early May 2009.” Notch is a video game developer that decided to have his main focus on creating Minecraft in 2009. The game was updated from Java Edition to the Classic Version, which is currently played today. There are also four different game modes in Minecraft: Creative, Survival, Hardcore, and Adventure. The objective of Minecraft in Survival mode (most popular game mode) is to appear in a new world and find resources to build shelter, collect food, and craft tools to help players survive. Minecraft is an excellent game because it allows players to collect materials/tools to be able to survive, which makes the game a lot of fun.

2. Fortnite

Fortnite was created by Epic Games on July 21, 2017. Epic Games is an “American company founded by CEO Tim Sweeney.” Tim Sweeney is a video game programmer and businessman. The objective of the game is to be the last person or team left alive in the round. There are up to 100 players in one Battle Royale game, and throughout the game, players must collect guns and materials to be able to survive. Fortnite is a good game because it allows players to experience challenges and use teamwork to win a Battle Royale!

1. Roblox        

Roblox is among many of the popular games played today. Roblox has been around since September 1st, 2006 and has gained more attention over the years. The objective of the game is that players have the ability to create their own worlds. Players also have the ability to play thousands of online games with their friends. One of the many great things about Roblox is that if you get bored of one game, you can play another game. Roblox is considered to be for all ages because there isn’t much violence at all in the game. Today, there are “…around 58.8 million active users each day worldwide.” Roblox is a great game because players can create and join multiple online worlds!

In conclusion, video games have changed our lives entirely these past couple years. They have gotten more complex and superior as of 2022. Hopefully in the future, there will be even better video games that will blow our minds!

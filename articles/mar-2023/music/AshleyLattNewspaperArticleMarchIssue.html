Could this music artist change the world?

By: Shaista, Ashley, Evalina

Who is this life-changing music artist?

Elizabeth Woolridge Grant, also known as Lana Del Rey, is an American singer, songwriter, and poet. She started singing at the age of 18. Furthermore, she got recognized and signed a music contract to Interscope Records and Polydor. Her managers are still the same but her producer is now Jack Antoff who also produces Taylor Swift’s music.  

Her first album

Her first album was called Born to Die. It was released on January 27, 2012. It included 12 songs, and soon became the fifth global best selling album of 2012. By 2014 it had sold seven million copies worldwide. Born to Die is still growing today and increasing in popularity. As of now, it has over 14 million copies sold worldwide. It was so successful because she expressed her emotions through her music. She conveyed herself through past experiences such as trauma that many women struggle through. That was a big reason why she gained popularity in the music industry. Her music is also famous for having notes of glamor, tragic romance and melancholia. It's also well known for its cinematic quality.

How is she changing the world?                                

Not only is Lana Del Rey allowing pop music to be gloomy, she is also allowing the listeners to connect to her music by making it a safe space. Many artists who are female don’t really express their feelings. Her target audience are usually young women who experienced love and the loss of their loved ones. Her music is changing the industry for years to come. Lana Del Rey has six Grammys nominations and won 32 awards with 96 nominations. She has also won a Visionary Award for Billboard Women in Music. She is still growing even more and everyone is excited to see what surprise Lana Del Rey has next.                                        

Lana Del Rey’s upcoming album

Lana Del Rey’s next album, “Did You Know That There's A Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd” will be released March 24th, 2023. Which is a reference to the tunnel under Ocean Blvd and how everyone forgot about it. The tunnel was very famous before but now it is forgotten. Furthermore, she is telling her audience to never forget her like the Ocean Blvd. She has already released two songs from the album and shared sixteen tracklists. More than millions of fans are awaiting for the release.

Conclusion

        As you can see, Lana Del Rey is a life-changing artist that has inspired millions and will continue to inspire more! In a magazine interview with Billie Ellish, Billie was talking about how Lana inspired her to be an artist. She has been collaborating with multiple artists like The Weeknd, Taylor Swift, Stevie Nicks, Playboi Carti, A$AP ROCKY, and more!

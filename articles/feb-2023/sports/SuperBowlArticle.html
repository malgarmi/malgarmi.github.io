Everything you need to know about Super Bowl LVII By: Evan Huang & Marleau Mendoza

Intro

Get ready for the most-watched American sport event, The Super Bowl! We’ll go over the 2 teams competing for the Vince Lombardi Trophy, who will be performing at the halftime show, and how you will be able to watch this big event. There can only be one team that will come on top to become the Super Bowl 57 Champions.

Teams

 The Kansas City Chiefs finished the season 14-3 and managed to clinch the 1 seed. They defeated the Jacksonville Jaguars 27-20 in the divisional round and advanced to their 5th straight AFC Championship game where they went on to defeat the Cincinnati Bengals 23-20. This will be the Chiefs’ third Super Bowl appearance in 4 years and fifth appearance all time (appeared in Super Bowls 1, 4, 54 and 55). The Chiefs are looking to win their third Super Bowl in franchise history.

The Philadelphia Eagles had the same regular season success as the Chiefs, also finishing the season 14-3 and clinching the 1 seed. They destroyed the New York Giants in the divisional round 38-7 and would then defeat the San Francisco 49ers in the NFC Championship game 31-7. This will be the Eagles 4th Super Bowl appearance all time (appeared in Super Bowls 15, 39 and 52) and they will be looking to win their 2nd ever Lombardi trophy.

Venue

On February 12, Super Bowl 57 is set to be played at State Farm Stadium in Glendale, Arizona, home of the Arizona Cardinals. This will be Arizona’s 2nd time hosting the Super Bowl, as the stadium last hosted back in 2015 (Super Bowl 49) where the New England Patriots defeated the Seattle Seahawks 28-24. According to ticketmaster.com, the price range for one ticket is from $5,816 to $19,940. In comparison, the first Super Bowl had an average ticket price of $12, which today costs around $100. If you are unable to go and watch the game at Arizona, don’t worry you can still watch the game at 3:30 p.m. PST. Foxsports.com states, “The Super Bowl will be available to be streamed live on the FOX Sports website and the FOX Sports App.”, and NFL.com states, “the Super Bowl will be available live on NFL +.”

Halftime Show

 The 9 time Grammy winner, Rihanna, will be performing at the Super Bowl Halftime. She is known for her popular songs Umbrella, Diamonds, Love the Way You Lie, This is What You Came For, and so many more songs. Rihanna has not released an album in 7 years. Many fans are excited to watch her perform, and according to cinemablend.com , “it will mark the first performance from the multi-Grammy winner in five years.”

The Halftime show happens after the 2nd quarter which is half of the game, and the Super Bowl halftime shows lasts from 12-15 minutes. Nbcsports.com says, “The first Super Bowl took place on Jan. 15, 1967, and the halftime show was performed by the University of Arizona Symphonic Marching Band”. There have been a variety of different halftime show performers such as The Weeknd, Lady Gaga, Bruno Mars, Katy Perry, Kendrick Lamar, Snoop Dogg, and many others. Most Super Bowl performances will have a guest appearance to perform with them but there is no confirmed guest yet.

You will also not want to miss the commercials. The commercials have famous celebrities advertising a company you wouldn’t expect. Such as Megan Thee Stallion and Charlie Puth in a Cheetos and Doritos commercial in 2022.

Conclusion 

After a long, hard-fought season, only two teams remain. The Eagles and the Chiefs will have to try their hardest to win the title of the Super Bowl LVII Champions. Along with that, Rihanna will be performing at the halftime show inside State Farm Stadium in Glendale, Arizona. Who will you be cheering for in the Super Bowl?

Top Five Valentine's Day Movies

By: Ashley and Evalina

        Every February 14, people would give chocolate and teddy bears to their significant other. Valentine's Day is enjoyed by people worldwide with family, friends, and partners. They might also watch Valentine’s day movies as a hangout. Today we will rank the top five Valentine’s day movies for you to watch.

Rank Five:

Gnomeo and Juliet is ranked as number five.

The movie is a spin-off of the story Romeo and Juliet. It’s about a pair of garden gnomes who are in love with each other. Although their owner despises one another, Gnomeo and Juliet secretly meet up with each other without their owner knowing. They have to keep finding a way to keep their love a secret from their family. It had lots of frog-eating scenes which was quite disturbing for children. The first 30 minutes of the movie was a detailed film of the sock puppets. What sucked about this movie was when two gnomes had a fight and one got crushed by a truck. It was unfortunate to see the gnome getting smashed into pieces.  I will have to give it a 6.5/10 because it was a knockoff version of Shakespeare.

Rank Four:  

The movie that is ranked number four is Princess and The Frog. Princess and The Frog is a movie about a girl, Tiana, who is a waitress who dreams of opening up a restaurant. Then she meets a  prince named Naveen, who was turned into a frog by an evil antagonist, Dr. Faciller. Prince Naveen mistook Tiana for a princess and kissed her in hopes to be back to normal. Sadly, Tiana also turns into a frog so now they have to go on an exciting experience to return to normal. This movie has representation for African-American girls. The voodoo magic was disturbing to some people. The movie is inspiring, so I will rate this an 8.5/10.

 

Rank Three:

The third movie out of the five is Be My Valentine, Charlie Brown. The main character, Charlie Brown, hopes to get a Valentine's day card from anyone. Meanwhile, a boy named Linus wanted to get the biggest Valentine’s gift for his beloved teacher. While Charlie and Linus are busy thinking about gifts, two side characters named Snoopy and Woodstock are busy having fun. The movie can teach kids/teenagers that it’s okay not to have a valentine. Overall, the movie was really wholesome and kid-friendly so I will rate this movie a 9/10.

Rank Two:

The second-best movie for Valentine’s day is Up. The film starts out with the protagonist, Carl meeting a girl with ginger hair and crooked teeth named Ellie. They quickly became friends and the couple grew old together. Sadly, something terrible happened to Ellie. Carl became a depressed and cold-hearted man until he met a kid named Russel. They soon have adventures together with a talking dog, Dug, and an exotic bird, Kevin, to achieve his wife’s dream of traveling to South America. The emotional appeal was very complex, like watching two elderly men fight for their lives was terrifying. This movie was really cute and sweet so I would rate it a 9.5/10.

Rank One:

The top and ultimate movie to watch for Valentine’s day are Shrek. An ogre, named Shrek, lived far away from a village in a swamp. Soon, there was an invasion of fairy tale characters that were banished from a kingdom ruled by Lord Farquaad. Shrek makes a deal with Lord Farquaad to rescue a princess named Fiona for his swamp to be back to normal. This movie is a really endearing Valentine’s Day movie because it is a love story about Shrek and Fiona. It had a lot of action and everything about this movie was complex in a very good way. I rate this a 10/10.

We ranked five Valentine’s day movies from number one as Shrek and number five as Gnomeo and Juliet. These movies are perfect for watching when you have some free time on Valentine’s day. This article is written to show you the best movie for an ideal date. Enjoy your thrilling movie night!

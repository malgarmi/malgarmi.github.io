Joe Biden: America’s Idol or America’s Downfall?

In the 2020 election, Joe Biden and his running mate Kamala Harris won against former president Trump and his running mate, Mike Pence. Since then, Joe Biden has been making some vital decisions for America. He has made students’ lives easier by reducing university fees, making it affordable for many families. On the other hand, it is believed that Joe Biden is hiding something from America. The FBI recently investigated Biden’s house and found some illegal documents. Is Joe Biden worthy of the title and power of America’s presidency ?

Who is Joe Biden?

Joseph Robinette Biden, Jr. (born November 20, 1942, in Scranton, Pennsylvania, U.S.) became America’s 46th president. Joe Biden graduated from the University of Delaware And Syracuse Law School. Later on, he served on the New Castle County Council. When Joe Biden was 29, he was elected to the United States Senate. Sadly, a week after this happy event, his wife, Neilia, and his daughter Naomi were killed in an accident. His two other sons, Hunter and Beau, were critically injured. On August 23, 2008, Biden was declared as Barack Obama’s running mate and soon became the 47th vice president.

Pros

First, Biden has strengthened labor and workers’ rights. Joe Biden supports Legislation like the PRO Act that will allow for secondary boycotting. According to Afl-Cio.org (American Federation of Labor and Congress of Industrial Organizations), This website is made by “America’s Reunion.”. It's a site that is explained by union members.  “President Biden used his bully pulpit to advance the causes of the labor movement, including calling for passage of the Protecting the Right to Organize (PRO) Act, supporting workers’ right to organize at Amazon and calling out Kellogg’s (Cereal company) corporate greed during an active strike.” Biden also has about 50 years of significant experience with politics. On Joe Biden’s first day in office, he fired Donald Trump’s National Labor Relations Board general counsel, Peter Robb. This clearly shows that he is on the workers’ side, not the corporation’s. He shows that he doesn’t just go

with the previous president’s Board general Counsel and is actually hiring a pro worker.

 Biden has also helped students like us with college fees. Department of Education analysis states that “typical undergraduate students with loans now graduate with nearly $25,000 in debt”. Biden makes student loans more manageable for borrowers by reducing the monthly payments by 50% for undergraduate students! He also announced forgiving the loan balance after you pay for ten years. This makes college fees more affordable for approximately 43 million students.

Cons

Biden might have made critical sacrifices for America, but the FBI recently found some mind-blowing information. The FBI tracked down classified documents at the Wilmington, Delaware, home of President Joe Biden during a search that lasted 13 hours at Biden’s house. This discovery was the fourth time since November that the FBI found classified info at Biden’s house. When the FBI last searched Biden’s house. Joe Biden’s personal Lawyer, Bob Bauer, stated, “the Department of Justice seized six items consisting of documents with classification markings and surrounding material.” Another occurrence with classified materials was encountered in Joe Biden’s house on December 20, 2022. This time they found it in Biden’s garage. Many other classified documents were found later on. The NY Post says “documents also reportedly include intelligence memos and material related to Iran, Ukraine, and the United Kingdom. They were mixed in with other personal papers belonging to the president, including funeral arrangements for Biden’s late son Beau, who died of brain cancer in 2015”. The citizens of America do not know why Joe Biden would do this, but they do know that the act of stealing classified documents is unacceptable.

Joe Biden is also crippling the American people’s personal finances. The CBO, also known as the Congressional Budget, has confirmed that the “levels of spending, debt, and taxes in the president’s budget will be the highest sustained levels in U.S. history, with spending increasing 62% and taxes increasing 80% over that of the previous ten years, making the crises that have defined the Biden Presidency…. Worse.” This information clearly shows that he is overspending. Not only that, but his out-of-control spending and catastrophic economic policies have made inflation reach its highest in 40 years! This inflation is causing lots of damage to the people; the increase in the cost of goods makes it hard to afford for low-income people, and average hourly earnings have decreased by 1.7%! Joe Biden’s mistake has created a chain of problems.

 

Conclusion

Joe Biden, an 80-year-old man, possibly America’s greatest or maybe even the worst president. Joe Biden has been making decisions for America ever since, affecting millions of Americans and immigrants. He has done good deeds, such as making college fees less expensive and affordable, but he has also done some trust-breaking actions, such as having classified documents in his house. Now it is up to you, is Joe Biden: America's idol, or is he America's downfall?

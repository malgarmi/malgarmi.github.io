Top 3 Snacks at Centerville

By: Shaista, Evalina, and Ashley

Introduction:

        Struggling to find the perfect snack? This article will talk about 3 delicious snacks that you can try! These snacks are easy to carry at school and you can share them with your friends. Today we will be rating these snacks based on how portable they are and how they taste.

 3: Barbeque Pringles:

     The barbeque flavor is a classic and tasted a lot like barbeque sauce from McDonald's. It is sweet but savory at the same time. They were crunchy and tangy. The flavor was strong but it didn’t overpower the crispy potato chip flavor. Overall, the taste of pringles are good but they could improve on the flavor. The can is also not that portable to carry around. The rating will be 7/10.

2: Chips Ahoy (Chewy version)    

    Chip Ahoy (Chewy Version) is a wonderful snack. The chocolate flavor is sometimes too powerful which makes the cookie unbalanced. The ratio of the chocolate and the cookie dough is off. The cookie was nice and soft but chewy at the same time.  It can be an on the go snack. It is softer than the original ones. We will rate this a 8.5/10.

1: Peanut M&M’s

The M&Ms were amazing! It is wonderful how you can taste the peanut and the chocolate in one bite. The more you eat the M&Ms the better it gets. It had a strong yet sweet smell of chocolate with a hint of peanut and was very crunchy. The peanut M&M is bigger than the regular one making it easier to chew. You can take these anywhere! It makes a wonderful snack to carry at school and at home. My rating is 10/10.

Conclusion:

As you can see we had rated the top 3 snacks, but our opinion may differ from yours which is okay! The snack tastes really good and can be carried around easily. You can share them with your family and friends. Furthermore, they all make a great movie snack.

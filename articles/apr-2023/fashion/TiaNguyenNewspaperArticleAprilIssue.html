Cargo Pants

Cargo pants were originally used and designed for military purposes as a way to store items in the various pockets. These pants were first worn by the British Armed Forces in 1938 and were introduced to the United States in the mid 1940s during World War II. Cargo pants soon gained more popularity as the veterans came home and they soon ended up in stores. They grew to be a popular fashion trend in the early 2000s and were brought back in the 2020s as part of the “Y2K” fashion trends (where 2000s fashion trends were brought back). Celebrities have started to wear cargo pants and they have caught on with many people. Due to cargo pants becoming fashionable, they have been made to be more loose fitting. Cargo pants are comfortable and can be worn just about anywhere.

Oversized Clothing

Oversized clothing was a popular style back in the 90s, but over the last 15 years people have enjoyed wearing clothing that was designed to be more slim and pleasing. But after covid and changing of fashion styles, baggy clothing has been brought back. With the loose fitting, they bring a lot of comfort and allow air to flow through freely. Oversized clothing is most likely connected to hip hop where most rappers dress in this type of style. There are several theories on how baggy clothing has become a trend, but as it has become more popular, celebrities and fashion influencers have started to wear this fashion style. They have played a major role in popularizing this type of fashion, and now more people wear this style. Oversized clothing can be worn as a casual look and in any type of weather.

Many teens focused brands like Zara, H&M, and Aeropostale have many cute hoodies in varying sizes. With varying sizes, you can choose the perfect size to bring you the comfort you need. At Centerville, many students tend to wear oversized hoodies due to their cozy fit. Large hoodies keep students warm and comfy as well as stylish.

Air Forces

Air forces are another popular brand of shoes. They are produced by Nike. Nike debuted them on basketball courts in 1982.  Their most recognizable color is white or gray. Air Forces, like Air Jordans, became iconic in the 2000s and still continue to be worn today. They are now a staple of many fashionable closets. They have been produced with low, mid, and high cuts. The low cut being the most popular style as it is the most comfortable.

Jeans

Denim was created in the 17th century in France. Throughout the years, denim was the working man's clothes. Now many styles have been made. The ones of note currently are baggy jeans, flared jeans, and ripped jeans. Some of these styles are interchangeable and have been combined. Flared jeans became popular in the 2000s. The wide cuts cause them to be more flattering and stylish than other styles. Baggy jeans became popular in the 2000s due to rappers. They continue to be used because they are more comfortable than other styles. Ripped jeans are also very popular. Any type of jeans can include rips, such as baggy jeans and flared jeans. Jeans go well with every outfit and should always be in your closet.

Conclusion

Fashion is unique to every individual. Clothes are a way for people to express themselves. For others, clothes are a way to feel comfortable. Cargo pants, oversized clothes, jeans, and air forces are the trending clothes of 2023. Many of these styles have come recently due to their flattering fits and style variability. Fashion comes with many different combinations of styles, and everyone has a unique kind of personality that connects with the way they dress.

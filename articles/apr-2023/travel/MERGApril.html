Three Amazing Summer Vacation Trips

By: Goku, Evan, Marleau, Ricky

Intro

        Summer is coming up soon, which means many people are going on vacation. There are many great places to go in the world but we chose these three vacation spots. The vacation spots have beautiful beaches to cool down in the hot summer weather, and amazing outdoor activities! These trips will change your life.

3rd: San Diego, California

San Diego is a wondrous city located in Southern California known for its refreshing weather, amazing nature, and its numerous tourist attractions. A round trip flight from San Francisco to San Diego lasts only 1 hour and 20 minutes and costs around $150-$330. If flying doesn't seem like a good option, a drive from the Bay Area to San Diego is approximately 8 hours. Once you do get to San Diego, there are so many places and activities to do here. You can go to the amazing beaches, or visit the San Diego Zoo. You can also go to Belmont Park, the San Diego Air & Space Museum, or the USS Midway Museum. The weather in San Diego in the summer is not that hot, as it rarely gets hotter than 80 degrees, which is good enough to enjoy the many outdoor activities here. As for clothing, any casual clothing is fine since it isn’t that hot, unless you want to enjoy the beaches.

2nd: Hawaii

        

Hawaii is known for its beautiful and wonderful beaches year round. Weather forecast for this year predicts that the weather during summer will be a warm 88°F. You should bring flip flops, t-shirts, shorts, a swimsuit, or any clothes to keep you cool and comfortable in the sun, and some sunscreen. A round trip from SFO to HNL (Daniel K. Inouye International Airport, Honolulu, Oahu) is around $350-$500. Hawaii also has 9 national parks with Hawaiʻi Volcanoes National Park having two of the most active volcanoes in the world right now. Hawai’i Volcanoes National Park is known for its beautiful views and hikes. The National Park is located on the island of Hawaii. There are also many water activities in Hawaii like snorkeling, kayaking, scuba diving, surfing, and even riding on dolphins. One of the easiest activities to learn is scuba diving and it's very rewarding because of the fascinating animals you’ll see. A souvenir you can get in Hawaii is a Lei that originated from Hawaii itself. A lei is like a necklace made commonly with flowers.

1st: Dubai

        Did you know Dubai is the 3rd richest country in the world? Dubai is known for its luxurious vacation spots. It takes 16 hours from San Francisco to Dubai during the flight. It costs approximately $1,500 for a round trip flight ticket . There are many beaches and fun activities (water parks and amusement parks) that you can do in Dubai. It is 90-100 degrees in Dubai. Since Dubai is hot during the summer, it's best to wear light cotton clothes. But don’t be afraid of the weather. Dubai is the home of the biggest mall on earth: The Dubai Mall. Dubai also has the world's tallest skyscraper. The Burj Khalifa reaches up to 828 meters tall (2716.5 feet)! The hotels are also cheaper during the summer in Dubai. There are tons of beaches in Dubai like Sufouh beach, Black palace beach, and the Marina beach. These are some of the many beaches in Dubai. Dubai has lots of water parks and has one of the best aquariums on earth. The “Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo” is an aquarium where you see many diverse species of animals. Dubai is an amazing place to visit and will be a wonderful time of your life!

Conclusion

Why stay in your home when you can go to these places? Tell your parents these trips will make them have the best time in their lives.These are once in a lifetime vacation spots. Go, explore the dolphin rides in Hawaii, the San Diego zoo, and the Burj Khalifa. These are just a few of the many awesome places on Earth. So instead of staying home, take a trip.

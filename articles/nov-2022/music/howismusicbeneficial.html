How is music beneficial to people?

                                   Written by Jarred Hidalgo and Isabella Edwards

                                

          We all listen to music, but do you know how it can benefit you?

        Anxiety

        Anxiety is something stressful that adults and even students experience in different situations. Studies have shown that tuning into music can help with calming down your nervous system and with repressed emotions. People often turn to relaxing music when feeling anxious or afraid because it helps them calm down and reduce their stress. So if you feel worried, uneasy, or distressed consider listening to some songs that you feel calm you down.

Memories

We listen to music that reminds us of a moment that impacted our lives in a memorable way, good or bad, because it helps us cope or move on for the better. When we hear a certain song after a while you feel nostalgic, and sometimes we even get emotional depending on what memory comes to mind. According to Petr Janata his initial study found that “music serves as a potent trigger for retrieving memories.”

Motivation

        Listening to songs that we enjoy helps motivate us to do various activities. Such as studying, cleaning, working, exercising, achieving our goals, and etc. Hearing a song we enjoy can really motivate us to achieve what we’re trying to do and can even help us do our best work when we’re feeling a bit anxious or scared. According to Florida National University, “Music is found to help people perform better in high-pressure situations.”

Mental health

        Music can be therapy and healing to people who are mentally suffering and help them recover and feel relaxed from whatever is making them feel that way. When we feel a negative emotion we often listen and relate to the lyrics more than the rhythm and beat. Often when we hear music and really pay attention to the lyrics, we can hear the artist talking about something that they’re most likely going through, and making a song about it to cope. That's the reason why we frequently hear songs about depression, heartbreaks, anger, addiction, etc.

        These are all the other ways music can benefit us. There are many more proven ways not stated in this article, but hopefull new information has been brought to mind on the topic of music.

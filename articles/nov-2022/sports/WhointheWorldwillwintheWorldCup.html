Introduction

Soccer is a sport famous all around the world and most of the world's population adores it. As the 2022 world cup is right around the corner (starting November 20)  many people are wondering which team will be the next world champions. There are a total of 32 nations that are participating in this event, some of them for example, are England, Iran, Netherlands, USA, Wales, Argentina, Saudi Arabia, and Denmark. In this article, we will provide predictions, data, and info about soccer and its top teams. This event will take place in Qatar this year, and happens once every 4 years! 

Why we Believe France will Win. 

Although Brazil is the favorite to win this year, we believe that France has the best chance to win the world cup. They have everything that a winning team needs.  The Les Blues teams has chemistry, a lot of talent and they have experience. France has a robust set of players that will be a deciding factor for them. Kylian Mbappe who helped France win their 2nd World cup will be a key element for France. Florent Torchut a L’Equipe, a reporter who was singing the praises of Mbappe says, “‘I think Kylian Mbappe has already shown since the beginning of his career that he is capable of extraordinary performances. Pelé himself praised him a few years ago at the time of the 2018 World Cup.”’ Pelé is considered by many to be the greatest soccer player ever. In our opinion, we believe that France will come out on top.

     

Pros and Cons of Visiting the World Cup,

        First, this may be the first and last World Cup to be played in the wintertime. Normally in previous years, the World Cup had been played during the summer season. However, according to WeatherSpark, summer temperature rose to 94°F - 99°F in Qatar hence it had been pushed back to winter time. Another reason it has been pushed back was that covid had taken over. Even though a flight ticket costs around $1,000 in Qatar, it won’t go wasted as you can visit many other attractions. Places such as the Doha Tower, Aspire Tower, Sidra Public Art, and Al Wajbah Fort are amazing places to visit if you are going to Qatar. Since there are going to be 32 nations playing in the World Cup this year, you could meet 32 different countries at that time.

        Some drawbacks may include pricing. Although improvements have been made to reduce these sorts of risks it may still happen. It's also a huge waste of money. In the picture below on fifa.com, a ticket varies from $80 to $5,000 depending on which match and category you opt for. The reason that it's a waste is you could also watch this online live without spending as much money as going there in person.

 

About the Teams Competing in the World Cup

The 2022 FIFA world cup has about 32 teams competing for the grand prize of 42 million dollars.  Every team is different, with different strategies and players. Here we will give a summary of some of the teams competing.  Starting with Group A, which includes Ecuador, Senegal, and the Netherlands, those teams seem to be on the same level with each other being mediocre. England placed top in Euro 2020 (a soccer/football championship) and Iran was considered the highest-ranking team in the World Cup 2020. The USA finished in 3rd place in the 1930s world cup but hasn't been doing well ever since.

For Wales, this is the first time they have qualified for the world cup since 1958. Their team isn't the best, only being able to qualify for the final tournament twice since 1958. Mexico's team during the 2018 world cup was able to beat Germany causing an upset. They have decent players but were kicked out of a world cup due to fielding underage players. Poland qualified for the world cup finals on 5 occasions despite not winning any. They shocked the world by beating England 1-0 in the 1974 world cup qualification. Saudi Arabia has won the AFC Asian cup 3 times and had qualified for the world cup 6 different times; they were also the 2nd Arab team in history to reach world cup team 16. In Argentina, they have a skilled and famed player Lionel Messi on their team which helps them win games. They also are the most successful team for the CONMEBOL–UEFA Cup of Champions.

Conclusion

        This year’s world cup is taking place in Qatar and many other countries are participating.  We predict France will emerge victorious because the team has chemistry, extraordinary skills, and a strong set of players which is everything a good team needs. Going to the world cup has cons, like the price, and pros, like visiting Qatar’s major landmarks. Lots of money is poured into the World Cup, and many countries participate. The diversity of the players and countries in the World Cup makes it fascinating and spectacular, so if possible, go to the World Cup.

Top 5 Air Jordans Releasing 2023

By Liliana, Gisele, and Ynez

Jordans are very popular in today's society, they have made it to the top 10 shoes of 2022. Almost everyone owns a pair of their own,  and because the year 2022 is coming to an end, we will be discussing the top 5 jordans that will be released in 2023.  

        

“Seafoams”

Jordan 4’s got very popular in 2020 when people had the Black Cat Jordan 4’s. Most people have a pair of Jordans since they are a famous shoe brand. The shoes look like they would go with any outfit, and the colorway was a top hit in 2021. These stylish jordans are releasing on February 24th, 2023, and would cost about $200-300! I am very excited for these shoes to come out!

“Lucky Greens”

These Jordan 3’s have to be one of my favorites. The new Jordan 3’s look was inspired by the Lucky Green Jordan 1’s that had been released in 2020. The shoe has a fresh, clean green look and the colors go excellent with the style of the shoe. They are selling in the summer of 2023, but we are unaware of when. They are going for about $200- 250. I think these shoes will be popular and many of people will wear them just like other Jordan 3’s.

“Black Elephants”

Jordan 1’s first became popular because they have always had such high-quality performances. I think the new Jordan 1’s Elephant print will be a big hit having only the price being around $140! The retail price ranges from $115-180. The release date is going to be June 23rd, 2023. The shoes were inspired by the Jordan 3’s, and I think they look good! Also, in 2016, there was a shoe called Jordan 1 High “Black Elephants” and got its inspiration from the Jordan 3’s!

                           

“Black and White Pandas”

 The Jordan 1’s were the first Jordans that came out and have been around since 1985. They were originally priced at only $65!  Today, Jordans can cost a lot of money, the average price ranges from $90-500 or even more depending on the type of Jordans. The Jordan 1’s High 85 “Panda” has a color of black/summit white and is retail priced at $200. I think teenagers will love this simple black-and-white design look for 2023!

“True Blues”

The Air Jordan 1’s High OG “True Blue” is a great choice. The High OG’s are true blue and white-cement gray. They are retail priced at $180. These pairs of jordans go with present-day style and look clean. The only downside is that they’re a bit pricey, but for the quality, it’s worth it! Jordan 1’s are known to be snug, allowing all-day wear whether it’s low, mid, or high rise.

Hopefully this inspired you to get a new pair of jordans! We hope you liked these jordans as much as we do, and we can’t wait to see if anyone gets them next year! The colorway and style of these Jordans would go phenomenal with any outfit.
